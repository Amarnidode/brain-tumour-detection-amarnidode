About Brain Tumors

Brain tumors are masses or growths of abnormal cells in the brain. Many different types of brain tumors exist. Some brain tumors are noncancerous (benign), and some brain tumors are cancerous (malignant).

Our system can detect and classify the following types of tumors:

  • Glioma - A type of tumor that occurs in the brain and spinal cord
  • Meningioma - A tumor that arises from the meninges â€” the membranes that surround the brain and spinal cord
  • Pituitary - A tumor that forms in the pituitary gland at the base of the brain
  • No Tumor - Normal brain tissue without tumor presence
