Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the tumor detection?

Our AI model has been trained on thousands of MRI scans and achieves high accuracy rates. However, it should be used as a supplementary tool and not as a replacement for professional medical diagnosis.

Is my data secure when I upload an MRI scan?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. All uploaded images are processed securely and are not shared with third parties. Images are only stored temporarily for processing purposes.

Can I use this tool on mobile devices?

Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works on desktop computers, tablets, and smartphones.

What types of brain tumors can the system detect?

Our system can detect and classify glioma, meningioma, and pituitary tumors, as well as identify scans with no tumor present.
